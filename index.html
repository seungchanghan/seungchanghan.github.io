About

Research at the boundary of chemistry and computation.

I am a Ph.D. student in materials science working across computational chemistry, surface science, and machine learning.

I am particularly interested in the dynamic electrode–electrolyte interface: how it changes during reaction, how those changes affect catalysis, and how data-driven atomistic models can help us observe it.
